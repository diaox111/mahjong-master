Rules of Mahjong Master

Mahjong Master is a sheep-a-sheep style stacking puzzle. The goal is simple: clear all tiles from the board.

The Basics

  • Tiles are stacked in overlapping layers. You can only tap the topmost tiles of each pile.
  • Tap a tile to add it to your 7-slot tray at the bottom.
  • When you have 3 matching tiles in the tray, they disappear automatically.
  • If the tray fills up with 7 tiles before you match, you lose.
  • Clear all tiles to win the level.

The Two Levels

  • Level 1 - Easy: 8 different mahjong tile types, 3 layers, always solvable.
  • Level 2 - Insane: 18 different tile types, 4 layers, sometimes unsolvable by design. Pure chaos.

Strategy Tips

  • Don't rush. The game has no time limit. Plan ahead.
  • Unblock buried tiles first. Removing a top tile exposes new options.
  • Use Move Out 3. When stuck, dump 3 tiles from the tray into the removed zone to free space.
  • Use Undo. Last click can be undone if you change your mind.
  • Look for triples in the corners and edges. Buried tiles become accessible when their covers are removed.
