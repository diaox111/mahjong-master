How to Play Mahjong Solitaire

1. Match Tiles

Click two identical free tiles to remove them from the board.

2. Free Tile

A tile is "free" if no tile is on top of it and at least one side is open.

3. Clear Board

Remove all 144 tiles to win the game.

Mahjong Strategy Tips

Start from the Top

Always work on the highest layers first to unlock more tiles.

Focus on Long Pairs First

Bamboo 1-9 and Characters 1-9 are hardest to match last. Remove them early.

Save Bonus Tiles

Flowers and Seasons don't have pairs—match them early.
